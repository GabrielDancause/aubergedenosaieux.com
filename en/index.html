Welcome to Les Éboulements

One of Quebec's most beautiful villages

Nestled in the heart of Charlevoix, Auberge de nos Aïeux offers its guests an incomparable view of the St. Lawrence River, Isle-aux-Coudres and the mountains. An enchanting and welcoming place where time slows down.
