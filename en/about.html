Auberge de nos Aïeux

Auberge de nos Aïeux is a now legendary institution in Les Éboulements. Open since the 1950s by the Tremblay family, it has built a reputation as a place where life is good — thanks to its warm atmosphere, communal table, and friendly conversations around the fireplace in winter and by the pool in summer.

Located on Route du Fleuve, the inn overlooks a breathtaking panorama of the St. Lawrence River, Isle-aux-Coudres and the Charlevoix mountains. A place where time seems to stand still and where each season reveals a new face of the region.

Our mission

To host travellers, nomads and freelancers in an enchanting, gathering place.

A place to create connections and better balance vacation and professional life, to the rhythm of the river and the Charlevoix seasons.

Upcoming project

Our vision

The camping and micro-houses site — we are currently designing a campground and a micro-house village to be located in the valley of the Auberge property, near the forest.

This innovative project will offer a unique nature lodging experience while preserving the charm and community spirit that have made the inn renowned for over seven decades.
