Gathering spaces

The inn, just like home

Our common areas are gathering spaces where travellers, families and remote workers meet. From the shared kitchen to the panoramic terrace, every corner is designed to make you feel at home.

Gathering

Common Spaces

Kitchen, dining room, stone fireplace lounge and shared bathrooms: our common spaces are designed to encourage connection and relaxation. Whether you're preparing a meal with friends or reading a book by the fire, you'll always find a welcoming spot.

Kitchen

Common Kitchen

Fully equipped with a stove, microwave, coffee maker, air fryer, dishes and utensils. We don't have a restaurant, but our kitchen is entirely at your disposal to prepare your best meals with local Charlevoix products.

Swim

Outdoor Pool

Open from June 15 to October 15, our outdoor pool is the perfect spot to cool off after a day of hiking or simply enjoy the Charlevoix sunshine with a view of the mountains.

Warmth

Indoor & Outdoor Fireplace

Enjoy our beautiful stone fireplace inside the lounge on cool evenings, or gather around the outdoor campfire under the stars. The perfect place to share stories with fellow travellers.

Remote work

Coworking

Work during the day, unwind in nature in the evening. With the fastest WiFi in the region and quiet spaces to work, the inn is the ideal place for remote workers and digital nomads.

Panorama

Outdoor Terrace

Happy hours, music nights and the best sunsets in Charlevoix. Our panoramic terrace offers a breathtaking view of the St. Lawrence River and Isle-aux-Coudres. Our visitors' favourite spot.
