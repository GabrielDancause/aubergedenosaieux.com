Apartments

The two-bedroom apartment is located on the second floor of the main building. It features two private bedrooms, two living rooms, a fully equipped kitchen and a full bathroom. A piece of history

Artistic Loft

Did you know that Auberge de nos Aïeux was known as a paradise for artists? The inn was the preferred destination for artists who would settle in to draw inspiration from the stunning views of Isle-aux-Coudres, Le Massif and the ferry crossing.

We have transformed this former workshop into a beautiful and peaceful artistic loft, filled with details created by our talented staff. Accommodates up to 4 guests, with a second double bed on the mezzanine and a private kitchen.

For vanlifers

Van Life

Several parking spaces available for converted vehicles (23 feet maximum). Renting a space gives you access to the same services as renting a room at the inn.

Open-air spaces with a community atmosphere. Please note that no water or electrical hookups are available at the parking spots. Access to washrooms, showers, pool, terrace and co-working.
